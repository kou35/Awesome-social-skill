# awesome-social-skills

[中文](README.md) | English

> A curated collection of character- and relationship-oriented AI skills, covering workplace scenarios, intimate relationships, cyber celebrities, public figures, and other interesting themes.  
> This repository gathers projects, templates, and ideas that distill memory, personality, habits, interaction styles, and lived experience into reusable AI skills, personas, and agents.  
> Whether a skill is practical, playful, or abstract-but-interesting, it is welcome to be discovered, collected, and explored here.

<a id="why"></a>

## Why This Repository Exists 🤔

- Character- and relationship-oriented skills are emerging rapidly, but they are scattered across different repositories and communities.
- This repository aims to organize them into an index that is easier to browse, extend, and explore.
- Whether you are looking for inspiration, entertainment, or a way to study how personas, skills, and agents are expressed, this is a good place to start.
